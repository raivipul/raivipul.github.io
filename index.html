Hi — Vipul Rai

I’m a PhD student at IIT Mandi, working on non-equilibrium statistical mechanics and stochastic thermodynamics. My research explores how information processing, especially in the context of Landauer erasure, links to heat dissipation. I also develop theoretical and computational models for animal homing behavior. Earlier, I studied open quantum systems, chaos theory, and transport in mesoscopic systems, all of which shape my broader interest in non equilibrium dynamics.
